About Our Guild

Welcome to the Historical Reenactment Group, where history comes alive through passionate dedication to authenticity and immersive storytelling. Our guild brings together enthusiasts from all walks of life who share a common love for historical periods ranging from ancient Rome to medieval Europe.

We meticulously research and recreate historical events, battles, and daily life, providing educational and entertaining experiences for audiences of all ages. Our members are trained in historical combat techniques, period crafts, and authentic social customs.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate historical heritage through accurate reenactments, educational programs, and community engagement. We strive to bridge the gap between past and present, making history accessible and exciting for modern audiences.

What We Do

  • Historical battle reenactments
  • Living history demonstrations
  • Educational workshops
  • Period craft demonstrations
  • Historical consultations
